What is NISMO?

We are Profesional

Since 1984, NISMO has been the embodiment of outstanding Nissan performance, inspired by the most unforgiving of proving ground, the "track race". An elite group of engineers and testers who have worked, driven, and won at every level take select Nissan vehicles and create NISMO vehicles that offer a unique, thrilling and authentic experience. With a name that comes from NISsan MOtorsport, you shouldn't expect anything less.
